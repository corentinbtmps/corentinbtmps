### 🚀 About me

I'm a **Lead Full-stack Developer** at the agency [Human To Computer](https://humantocomputer.com), where I own the architecture, quality and technical consistency of our projects — while still shipping code every day on the **TALL stack** (Tailwind, Alpine, Laravel, Livewire).

- 🧡 **10 years on the TALL stack**, 15+ years building for the web
- 🎨 **Design + dev double culture** — former graphic/web designer, so pixel-perfect and maintainable
- ⚡ Performance-obsessed: every project ships **Grade A (GTmetrix)** and **90+ PageSpeed**, even the complex ones
- 🧱 Co-author & maintainer of our internal Laravel/Livewire/Tailwind **starter kit** — ~90 models, 85+ Livewire components, Pest suite, GitHub Actions CI
- 🛠️ Creator of **[Laravel Vitals](https://laravel-vitals.com)** — open-source (MIT) Lighthouse audits inside your Laravel app
- 🌐 FR (native) · EN (full professional) · ES (working)
